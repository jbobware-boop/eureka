About the Federation

THE GOVERNING BODY
OF KENPO IN KENYA

The Kenya Kenpo Combat Federation is the official national governing body for Kenpo Karate and Combat Arts in Kenya, registered under the Sports Act and affiliated to global Kenpo bodies — Kenpo International with membership in 26 countries and 4 continents, and Ward Kenpo Schools International.

🥋
Belt Grading

Standardized grading system from White Belt through 10th Dan Black Belt, conducted by certified KKCF examiners.

📋
Member Licensing

Issue official KKCF member IDs, licences, and certificates recognized across affiliated dojos nationwide.

🏆
Competitions

Organize and sanction national and international Kenpo tournaments and championships throughout the year.

🎓
Instructor Certification

Train and certify Kenpo instructors to ensure quality teaching standards are upheld in every registered dojo.
